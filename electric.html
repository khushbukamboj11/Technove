Welcome to The Technove

At Technove Global, we believe that the future of transportation lies in sustainable, affordable, and innovative solutions. We are now extending our expertise into the electric vehicle (EV) space, bringing you a collection of affordable, high-quality electric scooters, made entirely in India. Our goal is to revolutionize the way you think about personal transportation, making it smarter, cleaner, and more efficient than ever before.

As we step into the world of electric mobility, we don’t just want to sell scooters. We want to redefine the way you travel – providing an eco-friendly, budget-friendly, and style-forward solution to get you from point A to point B. That’s why Technove Electric Scooters are designed for people who want to experience the joy of riding an electric scooter without compromising on performance, cost, or style.

Why Choose Technove Global Electric Scooters?

You may be wondering, what sets Technove apart from all the other electric scooter brands out there? The answer is simple: Innovation, Affordability, and Sustainability. Let’s explore what makes Technove different and why choosing us is the best decision you’ll make for your journey towards cleaner, smarter, and more sustainable transportation.

Our Mission

1

Affordability Without Compromise

At Technove, we believe in making electric scooters affordable for everyone. While many electric scooters on the market come with hefty price tags, we’ve worked hard to design scooters that deliver top-tier performance and cutting-edge features at a budget-friendly price. By focusing on efficient manufacturing and simplifying our processes, we’re able to pass on the savings to you without cutting corners on quality.

  • Affordable Pricing: Our scooters are among the most cost-effective in the industry, designed to fit into your budget without compromising on performance or style.
  • No Hidden Costs: What you see is what you get. Our prices are transparent, with no hidden fees or surprises.
2

Powered by Indian Innovation

Our scooters are 100% Made in India, ensuring that you’re not only supporting local manufacturing but also contributing to the growth of a sustainable, self-reliant India. Every Technove electric scooter is crafted with the highest quality materials and innovative technology to meet the needs of the modern Indian rider.

  • Support Local: By choosing Technove, you’re helping to support local jobs, innovation, and the ‘Make in India’ initiative.
  • Quality Assurance: Our scooters are designed and manufactured in India by skilled local engineers and technicians, ensuring the best quality at competitive prices.
3

Impressive Battery Technology

One of the most important aspects of any electric vehicle is its battery life and performance. At Technove, we don’t just settle for ordinary; we make sure that every scooter is equipped with a high-performance lithium-ion battery that guarantees a long-lasting ride.

  • 2-Year Battery Warranty: To give you peace of mind, we offer a 2-year warranty on the battery of all our electric scooters. Ride with confidence, knowing that you have the backing of Technove’s customer support team.
  • Fast Charging: Our scooters come with the latest fast-charging technology, so you can get back on the road quickly.
4

Sustainability at the Core

We understand that the future of transportation must be green, which is why we’ve made it our mission to provide a zero-emission, eco-friendly alternative to traditional vehicles.

  • Reduced Carbon Footprint: Riding a Technove electric scooter helps reduce the carbon emissions that come with conventional petrol or diesel vehicles, contributing to a cleaner, healthier environment.
  • Silent and Smooth Ride: Say goodbye to noisy, polluting engines and hello to the quiet hum of an electric motor. Technove scooters offer a silent, smooth, and enjoyable ride.
5

A Commitment to Excellence

We don’t just want you to buy a scooter from us – we want to provide you with a complete, satisfying experience. From the moment you first inquire about a Technove electric scooter to years after you’ve purchased one, we’re committed to offering unparalleled customer support and service.

  • Customer-Centric Support: Our dedicated support team is always ready to assist you, whether you need help with troubleshooting, servicing, or general inquiries.
  • Nationwide Service Network: No matter where you are in India, Technove’s extensive service network ensures that your scooter is well-maintained and ready to ride.
